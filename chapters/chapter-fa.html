Chapter 41

Fa

Fa is the 4th scale degree. In C, Fa is F.

This chapter adds Fa to your ear-training palette. Notice how it sits between Mi and Sol, but these drills mostly place it next to Sol, Re, and Do so you can hear its pull inside short melodies.

Text transcription

The sung targets in these exercises are: Sol–Fa–Re–Do; Fa–Sol–Fa–Re–Fa; and Re–Fa–Re–Do.
